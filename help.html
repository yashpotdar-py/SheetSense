SheetSense Help

chat Getting Started

SheetSense is an AI assistant that helps you analyze and understand your spreadsheet data. Here's how to get started:

  1. Click on the "SheetSense" menu in your Google Sheet
  2. Select "Open Chat" to launch the sidebar
  3. Enter your query in the chat box and press Enter or click the send button
  4. SheetSense will analyze your data and respond to your questions

psychology What You Can Ask

You can ask SheetSense various questions about your spreadsheet data, including:

  • Data Analysis: "Summarize this data" or "Find trends in column B"
  • Visualization Suggestions: "What's the best chart to visualize this data?"
  • Formula Help: "How do I calculate the average of values in column C that meet certain criteria?"
  • Data Cleaning: "Help me clean up this data"
Example: "What's the correlation between sales and marketing spend in this data?"
Example: "Create a formula to calculate the monthly growth rate based on columns B and C"

tips_and_updates Tips and Tricks

  • Be specific in your questions to get the most accurate responses
  • Use suggested questions to explore capabilities
  • You can press Enter to send a message, or Shift+Enter for a new line
  • Custom instructions in Settings can help the AI understand your specific needs
  • Use /clear in the chat to clear the conversation history

settings Configuring SheetSense

To configure SheetSense, select "Settings" from the SheetSense menu. You can:

  • API Key: Set or update your Gemini API key
  • Custom Instructions: Add specific instructions for the AI assistant
  • Chat History: Choose whether to save chat history between sessions
